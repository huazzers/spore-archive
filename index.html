Welcome to /spore-archive !

/spore-archive is an ongoing creative experiment which explores virtual taxidermy as a form of preserving digital artefacts at risk of going obsolete.

*

HOW TO USE THE ARCHIVE:

Click or Tab Select + hit [ENTER] on a log entry to view more details.

To navigate 3D model viewer, scroll to zoom; click + drag to rotate; hold [SHIFT] + mouse drag to pan.¬†
